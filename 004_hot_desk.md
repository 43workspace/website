### Hot desk

Hot desk is a non-reserved seating space. If you’d like to work out of 43 works without a private cabin, you can choose a hot desk.

### FAQ

**Q: What is a hot desk?**  
A: Hot desk is a non-reserved seating space. If you’d like to work out of 43 works without a private cabin, you can choose a hot desk.

**Q: How do I book a hot desk?**  
A: Log in to the 43 workspace app, and choose a day pass for hot-desking. You will receive a temporary access code to enter.

**Q: Can I book a hot desk only for a few hours?**  
A: No. A hot desk is available only as a day pass.

**Q: Can I choose the space I will get?**  
A: No. You can choose to take a seat where one is available. Ofcourse, you will have access to the internet and rest of the facilities.

**Q: Are meeting room credits included?**  
A: No. You can book it for an additional fee.
