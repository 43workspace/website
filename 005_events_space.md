### Events Space

Host workshops, trainings, small meetups or client sessions in our flexible events space. The room is equipped with seating, projector/TV, and high-speed internet.

### FAQ

**Q: What kind of events can I host?**  
A: 43 Events is suitable for tech meetups, non-profits events and corporate mixers.

**Q: What is the capacity of the space?**  
A: Approx 1200 sqft. Can seat 40-50 people comfortably.

**Q: What are the amenities available?**  
A: We have a large projection TV (85”), along with presentation A/V equipment and a stage area. Guest internet available. We can arrange for events to be live streamed.

The facility also has two washrooms. We can arrange for food & beverage for an extra cost. There are limited facilities in the basement for dispensing. We expect you to clean up after yourself. Plenty of street parking in the vicinity.

**Q: How can I book the space?**  
A: The space is available typically on weekends. Reservation is for 4 hours (typically) but available for full days. You can book the space using the 43 works app.
