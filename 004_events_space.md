### Events Space

Host workshops, trainings, small meetups or client sessions in our flexible events space. The room is equipped with seating, projector/TV, and high-speed internet.

### FAQ

**Q: What kind of events can I host?**  
A: The space is ideal for small workshops, training sessions, meetups and client presentations.

**Q: What is the capacity?**  
A: The events space comfortably accommodates small groups; contact us with your event details for a specific recommendation.

**Q: How do I book the events space?**  
A: Use the login-to-booking portal to view available slots and confirm your booking.

**Q: What does it cost?**  
A: Pricing depends on duration and timing. Log in to the booking portal to see current rates and available packages.
